# **Avatar 1 — The Cozy Soul Curator**
  
**Representative Person:** **Anna**, **Female**, **36 years old**
  
## **Executive Abstract**
  
Anna is a warm-hearted, emotionally sensitive woman who treats her home as her sanctuary. She seeks art that radiates warmth, softness, emotional connection, and calm — something that feels like “a hug on the wall.”

---
## **1. Core Profile**
- **Sex:** Female
    
- **Name:** Anna
    
- **Age:** 36
    
- **Family Status:** Married or in a long-term partnership; has one young child (3–8) or planning
    
- **Location:** Amsterdam, Vienna, Copenhagen, Munich
    
- **Occupation:** UX designer, psychologist, HR specialist, marketer, NGO project lead
    
- **Income Level:** Comfortable; prefers “practical luxury”
    
- **Lifestyle Keywords:** Candles, warm light, slow mornings, emotional balance, Pinterest inspiration
    

---
## **2. Habitus & Daily Life**
- Starts mornings quietly with coffee + soft light
    
- Overloaded emotionally from work/family logistics
    
- Home is her emotional base, not just shelter
    
- Follows gentle lifestyle, mental health & slow living accounts
    

---
## **3. Relationship with Art & Interiors**
- Visually intuitive, even if not educated in art
    
- Responds strongly to warm tones, soft lines, emotional themes
    
- Loves pieces that “feel alive” or “say something”
    
- Avoids cold, overly abstract or sterile works
    
  
_Strongly aligns with Shalysm’s research of love’s structure, warmth, simplicity and emotional presence_ 

---
## **4. Key Needs Solved by Mia Shaly Prints**
- Warm emotional expression on walls
    
- Gentle atmosphere: cozy, safe, calm
    
- Beautiful ready-to-hang solution
    
- Artwork with meaning and emotional depth
    
- Affirmation of family, closeness, connection
    

---
## **5. How She Discovers the Brand**
- Instagram Explore (keywords: “cozy art,” “warm home vibes,” “soft aesthetic”)
    
- Pinterest: “calm home color palettes,” “warm living room art”
    
- Shared content from friends
    
- Reels showing emotional transformation of a space
    

---
## **6. Objections & Counter-Messages**
  
### **Objection 1:**
###  **“What if it doesn’t fit my interior?”**
  
**Counter-message:** Show mockups and emphasize warm neutral tones that suit white, beige, oak, and soft-grain interiors.
  
### **Objection 2:**
###  **“Isn’t it a luxury?”**
  
**Counter-message:** Position prints as _long-term emotional anchors_ — small luxury with daily emotional benefit.
  
### **Objection 3:**
###  **“My partner might find it too sentimental.”**
  
**Counter-message:** Emphasize modern, clean, structured emotion — warmth without clichés.

---
## **7. Ideal Marketing Angles**
- “One print that makes your home feel like a hug.”
    
- “Art for evenings when you finally exhale.”
    
- “Where warmth and presence meet.”
    
  
### **Content Formats**
- Before/after reels
    
- Emotional text-over-light videos
    
- Carousel: “Soft home, calmer life”
    
- IG Stories with warm light + voiceover
    

---
## **8. Self-Critique**
  
**Strengths:** Extremely aligned with brand values; high emotional responsiveness.
**Weaknesses:** Segment is broad; could be split into “motherhood expansion” and “self-soothing” micro-segments.
